About

LiDAR-derived riverine susceptibility across Ljubljana, Savinja and Koper, with Koper coastal inundation and an ERA5-Land-style hydroclimate trigger. The riverine model combines HAND, TWI, elevation, slope, curvature, canopy interception and NDVI. The hydroclimate layer indicates temporal priming, not flood probability.

Data: Geodetska uprava RS — CLSS programme
